What's the story?

If you’ve seen Disney’s Encanto, you’ve probably noticed how food is an integral part of the Madrigal family’s day. Not only does Mirabel Madrigal sing about how her “mom Julieta can make you feel better with just one arepa,” but we see other foods make an appearance as well. For instance, during the scene of Isabel’s engagement dinner with the Guzmans, the two families sit down to a meal of Ajiaco Colombiano, a traditional Colombian soup made with chicken, potatoes, and corn. Whether you’re planning an Encanto birthday party or just want to sample some of the famous Encanto foods, I'm sharing a simple ajiaco soup recipe.

Steps to Make

Marinate the ground meat

  1. Place ground meat to a large bowl
  2. Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, corn starch, sugar, salt, and white pepper
  3. Knead and mix by hand while adding water
  4. Add mushrooms or green onions and thoroughly mix to distribute

Wrapping the wonton

  1. Use a small spoon to scoop the ground meat mixture
  2. Place the meal in the center of the wonton/dumpling wrapper
  3. Fold one corner diagonally towards the opposite corner
  4. Pinch the sides in to keep the meat secure (optional: use water to make it stick)

Making the soup

  1. Heat a large pot of water until it boils
  2. Drop the wontons in the boiling water
  3. Add baby spinach or napa cabbage to flavor the soup
  4. Wait until the wontons begin to float instead of sticking to the bottom
  5. Scoop and serve!
